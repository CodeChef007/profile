About Bryan Thompson

My philosophy

To cook is an expression it’s a journey along with it is stories, experiences and emotion, it’s not just a necessities. For me the way you embark on this journey is what shapes you in many ways there are no rules there are no rights or wrongs but what we must do is really believe and respect the experiences and how to express your self.

My favorite cook books:

  • HEAT by Billy Bulford
  • Coi by Daniel Patterson
  • The art of fermentation by Sandor Ellix Katz
